The Heart Behind Heartsnaps

Every magnet is printed, cut and pressed by hand — made to order by the Reid family in Forbes, NSW.

After being diagnosed with a congenital heart condition that changed everything, I found myself unable to continue in my previous career. But sometimes life's biggest challenges lead to our greatest opportunities.

I've always loved crafting and creating with my hands. Making photo magnets started as something I did for family and friends — watching their faces light up seeing their precious memories transformed into something they could display every day.

Now, from our family home in Forbes, NSW, I pour my heart into every single magnet I create. Each one is printed, hand-cut and pressed right here at our kitchen table. When you order from Heartsnaps, you're not just getting a product — you're supporting a real family and a small, regional Australian business.

Thank you for being part of our story. — Mandy & the Reid family
