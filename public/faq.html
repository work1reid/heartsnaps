Photos & Quality

Close-up portraits work best — faces should fill most of the frame. Use original photos from your camera roll, not screenshots or social media downloads (which are compressed and blurry when printed). Well-lit photos with natural daylight print beautifully.

Read our full photo guide for detailed tips.

We don't recommend it. Social media platforms compress images heavily — an Instagram photo is often only 30-40% of the original quality. Instead, find the original in your camera roll, or ask the photographer for the high-res file.
