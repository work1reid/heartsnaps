Refund & Returns Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Our Guarantee

At Heartsnaps, every magnet is handmade to order by the Reid family. We take pride in our work and want you to be completely happy with your purchase. If something isn't right, we'll make it right.

Damaged or Defective Orders

If your magnets arrive damaged or defective, please contact us within 7 days of receiving your order. We will either:

  • Reprint and resend the affected magnets at no charge, or
  • Issue a full refund for the affected items

Please include a photo of the damaged item(s) when you contact us so we can resolve your issue as quickly as possible.

Incorrect Orders

If we've made an error with your order (wrong photos, wrong quantity, etc.), contact us within 7 days and we will reprint and resend the correct order at no cost to you.

Change of Mind

Because every magnet is custom-made to order using your photos, we are unable to offer refunds for change of mind. Each magnet is individually printed, hand-cut and pressed specifically for your order and cannot be resold.

Photo Quality

The quality of your magnets depends on the quality of the photos you upload. We print exactly what you send us. If you're unsure whether a photo will print well, feel free to contact us before placing your order and we'll let you know.

Cancellations

If you need to cancel your order, please contact us as soon as possible. If we haven't started making your magnets yet, we'll issue a full refund. If production has already begun, we may not be able to cancel, but we'll do our best to work with you.

Shipping Issues

If your order hasn't arrived within 14 business days (for Australian deliveries), please contact us and we will investigate with Australia Post. If your order is confirmed lost in transit, we will reprint and resend your magnets at no charge.
