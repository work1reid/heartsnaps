Wedding Magnet FAQs

Can I use the same photo for all magnets?

Yes — that's the most popular choice for weddings. Upload one photo and set the quantity you need. All magnets will be identical.

Can I use different photos?

Absolutely. Some couples use different photos for each table, or different photos for different groups of guests. Just let us know in the order notes.

How far in advance should I order?

At least 3 weeks before your wedding date. This gives us 5-7 business days for production, 3-5 days for shipping, and a week's buffer for peace of mind.

Can you add text or our names to the magnet?

We print exactly what you upload. If you'd like text on the magnet, add it to your image before uploading using any photo editor. Most couples use a clean photo without text.

What size are the magnets?

63.5mm x 63.5mm (about the size of a large Post-it note). Square format, printed on premium photo paper with a durable laminate finish and strong magnetic backing.

What if I need more than 200?

Email us at heartsnaps2871@gmail.com or call 0421 191 476 and we'll put together a custom quote for larger orders.

What's your refund policy?

If your magnets arrive damaged or aren't up to standard, we'll reprint and reship at no charge. We want you to be completely happy. Full refund policy here.
