Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Who We Are

Heartsnaps is a small family business operated by the Reid family from Forbes, NSW, Australia. We make custom photo magnets by hand from our family home.

What Information We Collect

When you place an order with us, we collect:

  • Contact details: Your name, phone number, and email address (if provided)
  • Delivery address: If you choose delivery rather than pickup
  • Photos: The images you upload for your magnets
  • Payment information: Processed securely through Stripe — we never see or store your card details
  • Order details: What you ordered, quantities, and any notes or gift messages

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Make and deliver your magnets
  • Contact you about your order (e.g. shipping updates, questions about your photos)
  • Process your payment through Stripe
  • Improve our service

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your Photos

The photos you upload are used solely to create your magnets. We store them securely during production and for a reasonable period afterwards in case you need a reprint. We do not use your photos for marketing, social media, or any other purpose without your explicit permission.

Payment Security

All payments are processed through Stripe, a PCI-compliant payment processor. Your credit card details are encrypted and handled entirely by Stripe. We never have access to your full card number.

Cookies & Analytics

Our website uses Google Analytics to help us understand how visitors use our site (e.g. which pages are most popular). Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous usage data. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

We also use essential cookies to keep our website functioning (e.g. maintaining your session while you place an order).

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our business:

  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Supabase — secure data storage
  • Google Analytics — anonymous website analytics
  • Vercel — website hosting

Each of these services has their own privacy policy and handles data in accordance with applicable laws.

Your Rights

Under Australian privacy law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request corrections to your personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Ask us what data we have collected about you

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

Data Retention

We retain your order information and contact details for as long as necessary to fulfil your order and provide customer support. If you'd like your data deleted, contact us and we'll remove it within 30 days.
