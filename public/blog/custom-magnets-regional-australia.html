Photo Magnets for Regional and Rural Australia: We Ship Everywhere

March 2026 5 min read About Us
🌾

Every other custom magnet company in Australia is based in a capital city. Melbourne. Sydney. Brisbane. They ship from warehouses in industrial estates and their websites are designed for city customers.

We're in Forbes. Population about 8,000. Central West NSW. The nearest major city is a three-hour drive. We know what it's like to live regionally, because we do.

Why That Matters

If you live in a regional or rural town, you already know the drill. The gift shop closed years ago. The nearest decent shopping centre is an hour or more away. When you want something personalised or meaningful, you order it online — and hope the company actually ships to you reliably.

We get it, because that's our life too.

Heartsnaps was started by the Reid family right here in Forbes. We make every magnet by hand in our home. We post from the Forbes Post Office. And we ship to every postcode in Australia — regional, rural, and remote — for the same flat rate.

Our Shipping Promise

  • $8 flat-rate shipping — everywhere in Australia. Same price whether you're in Parramatta or Parkes, Bondi or Bourke.
  • Free shipping on orders over $350
  • Free pickup for locals in Forbes and surrounding towns
  • Every order tracked through Australia Post

From Forbes to Your Fridge

Forbes is in the heart of the Central West — surrounded by Parkes, Orange, Cowra, and Condobolin. It's farming country, it's small-town Australia, and it's home.

When you order from Heartsnaps, your magnets are made in a family home, not a factory. They're posted from a country town, not an anonymous fulfilment centre. And they arrive at your door with the same care whether you're in a capital city apartment or a farmhouse on a dirt road.

We've sent magnets to:

  • Every state and territory in Australia
  • Towns most delivery services charge extra for
  • PO boxes, rural addresses, and care-of addresses
  • Retirement villages and aged care facilities

Our $8 flat-rate doesn't change based on where you live. We don't add regional surcharges. We don't exclude postcodes.

Supporting a Regional Small Business

When you order from Heartsnaps, you're supporting a family in regional NSW. Not a venture-backed startup. Not a franchise. Not a Shopify dropshipper who never touches the product.

Every dollar stays in our community. The paper we print on, the packaging we use, the trips to the post office — it all happens here in Forbes.

We started Heartsnaps after facing some health challenges as a family. Making magnets gave us a way to build something meaningful from home, at our own pace, in our own community. It's honest work and we love doing it.

Free Pickup for Locals

If you're in or around Forbes, you can choose free pickup when you order. No shipping cost, no waiting for delivery. We'll let you know when your magnets are ready and you can collect them at a time that suits you.

We've had customers drive from Parkes, Orange, Cowra, and even further to pick up their orders — especially for larger wedding or bulk orders. You're always welcome.

A Gift That Gets There

One of the best things about photo magnets as gifts is that you don't need to be in the same town — or even the same state — to give them. When your mum is in Dubbo and you're in Melbourne, you can order a set of the grandkids' photos and have them delivered straight to her door.

Every order includes tracking, so you'll know exactly when the surprise arrives.

We send magnets to a lot of grandparents in regional towns. It's usually their grandchildren in the city ordering for them. The magnets become the highlight of the fridge and the first thing they show visitors.

Our Community

Forbes is a tight-knit community, and that spirit carries through to how we run Heartsnaps. When you call us, you get a real person. When you email, you get a real reply. When something goes wrong, we fix it — because that's how you do business in a small town.

We're proud to be a Forbes business. We're proud to ship from the Central West. And we're proud to make something by hand that ends up on fridges all across Australia.
