The Heartsnaps Process: What Happens After You Order

March 2026 5 min read Behind the Scenes
🛠️

When you order from Heartsnaps, your magnets aren't pulled from a warehouse shelf or printed by an automated machine in a factory. Every single magnet is made by hand, by our family, in our home in Forbes, NSW.

Here's exactly what happens from the moment you click "order" to the moment your magnets arrive at your door.

Step 1: You Place Your Order

You upload your photos, choose your quantity, and checkout through our secure Stripe payment system. We never see or store your card details — that's all handled by Stripe, the same payment system used by companies like Shopify and Amazon.

As soon as your order goes through, we get a notification. Usually within the hour, we're already looking at your photos.

Step 2: We Review Your Photos

This is something most magnet companies don't do — and it's one of the most important things we do.

Before printing anything, we look at every photo individually. We're checking for:

  • Resolution: Is the image sharp enough to print clearly at magnet size?
  • Lighting: Are faces visible and well-lit, or is the photo too dark?
  • Composition: Will the important parts of the photo show well in a 63.5mm square?
  • Colour: Do the colours look natural, or has a filter made them look odd in print?

If something doesn't look right, we'll contact you before printing. We'd rather have a quick conversation than send you a magnet you're not happy with.

"We got a message saying one of our photos might be too dark to print well. They suggested we send a different version. That kind of care is why we'll order from them again." — Lisa, Sydney

Step 3: Printing

Once we're happy with the photos, we print them using our high-quality printer right here in our home. We use premium photo paper that produces vibrant, accurate colours with sharp detail.

Each photo is printed individually — we don't batch hundreds of orders together. Your photos get our full attention.

We do test prints regularly to make sure our colours are calibrated and the output is consistently excellent.

Step 4: Cutting

Every magnet is cut by hand to our standard 63.5mm x 63.5mm size. We use precision tools to make sure each cut is clean and straight. No rough edges, no uneven borders.

This step is where the handmade difference really shows. A machine cuts fast but doesn't notice imperfections. We do.

Step 5: Pressing

The printed photo is laminated for durability and then pressed onto a high-quality magnetic backing. This is what gives your magnet its strength — it needs to hold firmly to any fridge or magnetic surface without sliding.

We test the magnetic hold on every batch. If a magnet doesn't stick strongly enough, it doesn't go out.

Step 6: Quality Check

Before packaging, every magnet gets a final inspection. We check:

  • Print quality — colours are accurate, no streaks or banding
  • Cut quality — edges are clean and even
  • Magnetic strength — it holds firmly when placed on a surface
  • Lamination — smooth, no bubbles or peeling edges
  • Overall appearance — does this magnet look like something we'd be proud to put our name on?

If anything isn't up to standard, we remake it. Simple as that.

Step 7: Packaging

Your magnets are carefully packaged to prevent any damage during shipping. We use rigid mailers so your magnets arrive flat and pristine — no bending, no scratches.

If you've added a gift message, we include a handwritten note inside the package. These small touches matter to us.

Step 8: Shipping

We ship through Australia Post with tracking. Every order gets a tracking number that you can use on our Track Order page or directly through Australia Post.

Shipping Details

  • Flat-rate shipping: $8 anywhere in Australia
  • Free shipping: On orders over $350
  • Free pickup: Available for locals in Forbes, NSW
  • Delivery time: Usually 3-5 business days from dispatch
  • Total turnaround: 5-7 business days from order to dispatch, plus delivery time

Step 9: Your Magnets Arrive

Open the package. Take out your magnets. Put them on the fridge.

That moment — seeing your photo as a real, physical magnet for the first time — is our favourite part. It's why we do this.

What If Something Goes Wrong?

We're human, and occasionally things don't go perfectly. Here's how we handle it:

  • Quality issue: If your magnet arrives damaged or the print quality isn't right, we'll remake and reship at no cost. Just send us a photo of the issue.
  • Lost in post: If your tracking shows the package isn't moving or it's been too long, get in touch. We'll work with Australia Post and sort it out.
  • Not happy: If you're not satisfied for any reason, reach out. We're a family business — we want you to be happy, and we'll do what it takes to get there.

You can reach us at 0421 191 476 or heartsnaps2871@gmail.com. You'll get a real person — usually the same person who made your magnets.
